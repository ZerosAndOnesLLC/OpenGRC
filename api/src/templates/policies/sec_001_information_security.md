**Category:** Security

## 1. Purpose

This Information Security Policy establishes the framework for protecting [Organization Name]'s information assets from all threats, whether internal or external, deliberate or accidental. This policy ensures the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information while supporting the organization's business objectives.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, consultants, temporary workers, and other personnel
- All information assets owned, leased, or managed by the organization
- All information systems, networks, and applications
- All locations where organizational information is processed, stored, or transmitted
- Third parties who have access to organizational information or systems

## 3. Policy Statements

### 3.1 Information Security Governance

- The organization shall establish and maintain an information security program led by designated security leadership
- Information security objectives shall be aligned with business objectives and regulatory requirements
- Security roles and responsibilities shall be clearly defined and communicated
- The information security program shall be reviewed at least annually for effectiveness
- Management shall demonstrate commitment to information security through adequate resource allocation

### 3.2 Risk Management

- Information security risks shall be identified, assessed, and treated in accordance with the Risk Management Policy
- Risk assessments shall be conducted at least annually and upon significant changes
- Risk treatment decisions shall be documented and approved by appropriate management
- Residual risks shall be accepted only with documented approval from risk owners

### 3.3 Asset Management

- All information assets shall be identified, classified, and inventoried
- Asset owners shall be assigned for all critical information assets
- Information assets shall be classified according to the Data Classification Policy
- Asset handling procedures shall be implemented based on classification level

### 3.4 Access Control

- Access to information systems shall be granted based on the principle of least privilege
- User access shall be reviewed at least quarterly
- Strong authentication mechanisms shall be required for all systems
- Access shall be revoked immediately upon termination of employment or contract

### 3.5 Security Operations

- Security monitoring shall be implemented for all critical systems
- Security events shall be logged, monitored, and analyzed
- Incident response procedures shall be maintained and tested
- Vulnerability management processes shall be implemented

### 3.6 Compliance

- Compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and contractual obligations shall be maintained
- Regular assessments shall be conducted to verify compliance
- Non-compliance shall be documented, reported, and remediated

## 4. Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|-----------------|
| Executive Management | Approve security policies, allocate resources, set security direction |
| CISO/Security Director | Develop and maintain security program, report on security posture |
| IT Management | Implement security controls, manage security operations |
| Department Managers | Ensure compliance within departments, identify assets and risks |
| All Employees | Follow security policies, report incidents, complete training |
| Internal Audit | Assess security controls, verify compliance |

## 5. Compliance

### 5.1 Monitoring
- Security metrics shall be collected and reported monthly
- Compliance audits shall be conducted at least annually
- Security assessments shall be performed quarterly

### 5.2 Enforcement
- Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination
- Suspected violations shall be reported to the Security team
- All violations shall be investigated and documented

## 6. Exceptions

- Exceptions to this policy must be documented and approved by the CISO
- Exception requests must include business justification and risk assessment
- Approved exceptions shall be reviewed quarterly
- Compensating controls may be required for approved exceptions

## 8. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| Information Asset | Any data, system, or resource that has value to the organization |
| Confidentiality | Ensuring information is accessible only to authorized individuals |
| Integrity | Ensuring information is accurate and complete |
| Availability | Ensuring information is accessible when needed |
| Risk | The potential for loss or damage when a threat exploits a vulnerability |
